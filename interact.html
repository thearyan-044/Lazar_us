Treasurer of Interact Club of Chennai. Contributed in being ranked as the best school interact club amongst 12 zones.

This prestigious recognition was achieved through exceptional leadership, innovative project management, and outstanding financial stewardship. As Treasurer, I played a crucial role in ensuring transparent financial operations, strategic budget allocation, and sustainable funding for community service initiatives. Our club stood out among 12 competitive zones by demonstrating measurable impact, efficient resource utilization, and consistent delivery of high-quality community projects. This achievement reflects our team's dedication to excellence and our commitment to making a meaningful difference in society through organized, well-funded initiatives.

Multi-Project Financial Management

Managed budgets for 6 Interact projects and led a personal basketball program at a government girls' school.

Successfully oversaw the financial planning and execution of six major Interact Club projects, ensuring each initiative met its objectives within allocated budgets. My responsibilities included detailed budget preparation, expense tracking, fund allocation optimization, and financial reporting to stakeholders. Additionally, I personally initiated and led a basketball coaching program at a local government girls' school, providing underprivileged students with sports training, equipment, and mentorship. This program not only promoted physical fitness and teamwork but also empowered young girls to pursue their athletic potential, creating lasting positive impact in their lives and building stronger community connections.

✦ Flagship Initiative

SOLAR
GRID PROJECT

As Interact Club Treasurer, I spearheaded the fundraising and financial management for a landmark solar grid installation project in Kanchipuram, raising over ₹1 crore. This initiative provided clean, renewable energy access to rural communities that previously lacked reliable electricity.

The project exemplified the power of community-driven sustainable development — combining financial acumen, community engagement, and environmental impact into one of the most ambitious projects our club had undertaken.
