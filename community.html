GOVERNMENT SCHOOL
CHARITY
SPORTS DAY

Organizing and participating in charity sports day events at government schools to promote physical fitness, teamwork, and community engagement among underprivileged students. These events bring joy, motivation, and educational resources to deserving children.

Documentation · Government School Sports Day

Charity Sports Day — Government School, Chennai

Students racing on the track during the charity sports day — competing, celebrating, and building community spirit.

Event Highlights

  • Organized sports competitions for 200+ students
  • Distributed sports equipment and supplies
  • Provided meals and refreshments
  • Awards ceremony for all participants

Community Impact

This initiative brought together students, teachers, and volunteers to create memorable experiences while promoting health, education, and community spirit. The event encouraged students to pursue sports and stay active.

Students racing on the track during the charity sports day — competing, celebrating, and building community spirit.

Personally initiated and led a basketball coaching program for underprivileged girls at a local government school, providing sports training, equipment, and mentorship.

The initiative empowered young girls to pursue their athletic potential and built stronger community connections through the universal language of sport — creating lasting positive impact in their lives.
